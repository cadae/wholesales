About Our Cooking Oils

At SCZ Group, we offer a diverse range of high-quality cooking oils sourced from trusted producers. Our selection includes vegetable oils, olive oils, specialized blending oils, and more to serve various culinary applications. All our products are selected for their purity, consistent quality, and excellent performance in commercial kitchens.

Our Cooking Oil Selection

Vegetable Oil

Our vegetable oil is a versatile cooking solution ideal for frying, baking, and general cooking purposes. It has a neutral flavor that doesn't overpower the taste of your dishes and a high smoke point that makes it suitable for high-temperature cooking.

  • Available Sizes: 5L, 10L, 20L
  • Packaging: BPA-free plastic containers
  • Shelf Life: 12-18 months
  • Best For: Deep frying, sautéing, baking

Olive Oil

Our premium olive oil is rich in flavor and nutritional value. Sourced from quality olive producers, it brings a distinctive taste to salads, marinades, and low to medium-heat cooking. We offer both extra virgin and pure olive oil varieties.

  • Available Sizes: 1L, 3L, 5L
  • Packaging: Glass bottles and tins

Canola Oil

Our canola oil is a heart-healthy option with low saturated fat content. It has a light flavor and a high smoke point, making it excellent for various cooking methods. This oil is preferred by establishments looking for healthier cooking alternatives.

  • Available Sizes: 5L, 10L, 20L
  • Packaging: BPA-free plastic containers
