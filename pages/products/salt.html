Salt Products

Essential seasoning and preservative solutions for food businesses

About Our Salt Products

SCZ Group supplies a comprehensive range of high-quality salt products for food service, food manufacturing, and industrial applications. Our salt products come in various grain sizes and formulations to meet diverse culinary and preservation needs. From standard table salt to specialty sea salts, our selection provides essential flavoring and preservative solutions for food businesses of all sizes.

Our Salt Selection

Table Salt

Our refined table salt is perfect for everyday cooking and seasoning. Available in iodized and non-iodized varieties, it dissolves quickly and provides consistent flavor enhancement for all food applications.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 25kg
  • Packaging: Moisture-resistant packaging
  • Shelf Life: 36 months
  • Best For: General cooking, baking, table use

Sea Salt

Our sea salt is harvested from evaporated seawater, retaining natural minerals that provide a complex flavor profile. Available in fine and coarse grades, it's perfect for gourmet cooking and finishing dishes.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 20kg
  • Packaging: Resealable bags and bulk options
  • Shelf Life: 36 months
  • Best For: Gourmet cooking, finishing, preserving
Request Quote

Kosher Salt

Our kosher salt features larger, flaky crystals that adhere well to food surfaces. It's ideal for koshering meat, brining, and general cooking where controlled seasoning is important. Its coarse texture makes it easy to pinch and sprinkle.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 20kg
  • Packaging: Food-grade containers
  • Shelf Life: 36 months
  • Best For: Koshering, brining, cooking

Industrial Grade Salt

Our industrial grade salt is designed for commercial food processing applications such as curing, preserving, and fermentation. It meets strict quality standards for consistency and purity.

  • Available Sizes: 25kg, 50kg, bulk options
  • Packaging: Heavy-duty bags, bulk containers
  • Shelf Life: 36+ months
