Sugar Products

Premium quality sweeteners for all your food and beverage needs

About Our Sugar Products

SCZ Group offers a comprehensive range of high-quality sugar products for food service, bakeries, beverage manufacturers, and other food processing businesses. Our sugar products are sourced from leading refineries, ensuring consistent quality and granulation. Whether you need standard white sugar, brown sugar, or specialty sugars, we have options to suit all your culinary and production needs.

Our Sugar Selection

White Sugar

Our refined white sugar is perfect for baking, beverages, and general food production. It dissolves quickly and completely, making it ideal for both hot and cold applications. Available in various granulation sizes to meet specific production needs.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 25kg, 50kg
  • Packaging: Paper bags and bulk options
  • Shelf Life: 24 months when stored properly
  • Best For: Baking, beverages, general sweetening

Brown Sugar

Our brown sugar has a rich molasses flavor that adds depth to baked goods and sauces. Available in light and dark varieties, it's perfect for recipes that call for a more complex sweetness and moisture retention.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 25kg
  • Packaging: Moisture-resistant bags
  • Shelf Life: 18 months
  • Best For: Baking, sauces, marinades
Request Quote

Caster Sugar

Our fine-grained caster sugar dissolves quickly, making it perfect for meringues, mousses, and cold beverages. Its consistency is ideal for recipes where regular granulated sugar would be too coarse.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 25kg
  • Packaging: Paper bags
  • Shelf Life: 24 months
  • Best For: Meringues, desserts, beverages
Request Quote

Icing Sugar

Our icing sugar (confectioner's sugar) is finely ground and perfect for frostings, glazes, and dusting desserts. It contains a small amount of anti-caking agent to prevent clumping and ensure smooth consistency.

  • Available Sizes: 1kg, 5kg, 20kg
  • Packaging: Moisture-resistant packaging
  • Shelf Life: 18 months
  • Best For: Frostings, glazes, dusting
